Yew



FACTS

Fruit is a bright red, fleshy, translucent cup surrounding a large seed. The red pulp of the fruit is not particularly toxic, but the seed must be avoided. Leaves, bark and seeds of the plant contain taxine and thus are deadly. Symptoms are nausea, circulatory failure and depressed respiration



SAFETY

First aid is to have the victim vomit Contact a doctor to treat for alkaloids and circulatory failure
