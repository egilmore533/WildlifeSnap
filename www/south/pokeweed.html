Pokeweed



FACTS

Symptoms are a burning sensation in the mouth and throat, nausea, vomiting and diarrhea A weakened pulse, drowsiness and impaired vision precede death in extreme cases The root is the most poisonous part of the plant, but mature stems and leaves also contain small amounts of the poison The pulp of the fruit is not poisonous and the young shoots (up to 6 inches high) are an excellent pot herb when properly prepared



SAFETY

First aid is to have the victim vomit Contact a doctor for circulatory and respiratory depression Contact Poison Control at 1 (800) 222-1222 after suspected poisoning
