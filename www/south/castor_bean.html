Castor Bean



FACTS

Seeds contain toxic substance called ricin, which can be extremely deadly Even infinitesimal amounts of this toxin can result in death Children are specifically prone to poisoning from this plant Symptoms are nausea, vomiting, intestinal cramps, convulsions, and often death



SAFETY

First aid for ricin poisoning is to make the victim vomit Contact Poison Control at 1 (800) 222-1222 immediately after suspected poisoning
