Nightshade



FACTS

All parts of this plant are very poisonous, but the most dangerous part are the berries which have a sweet taste and children can mistake them for overripe cherries. Two to four berries are fatal for a child; ten to twenty for an adult. Symptoms initially include a red face, dry mouth, dilated pupils, and quickened pulse. Symptoms progress to heart arrhythmia, tremors, hallucinations, paranoia, cramping, and death



SAFETY

Contact Poison Control at 1 (800) 222-1222 immediately after suspected poisoning
