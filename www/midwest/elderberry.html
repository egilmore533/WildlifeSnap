Elderberry



FACTS

Fruit is dangerous when consumed raw or incorrectly cooked. Some use cooked elderberry for medicinal or consumption purposes. Bark and leaves are poisonous regardless. Flowers and plant look extremely similar to Water Hemlock which is deadly. Can result in nausea, headache, vomiting, diarrhea, or other neurological problems



SAFETY

Only consume elderberry if you know how to properly prepare it. Consult your doctor before using it for medicinal purposes
