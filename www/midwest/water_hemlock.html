Water Hemlock



FACTS

Active poison is a resinous material called cicutoxin that appears yellowish and oil-like with a sweetish taste exists primarily in the roots of the plant. Can be easily mistaken for several other plants. Symptoms of poisoning are nausea, dilated pupils, vomiting, delirium, violent convulsions, and death.



SAFETY

Contact Poison Control at 1 (800) 222-1222 immediately after suspected poisoning
